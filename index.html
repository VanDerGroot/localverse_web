Collaboratively, we are Localverse.

Our vision is: Everyone, powerful.

For people to gain real choice locally about their own livelihoods and future.

First step is helping local businesses to thrive and flourish. In a sturdy bloom.

We'll test every step with a business model based on a local and open ethos.

Localverse will be our platform (it's yours too), for testing and building tools to drive toward our vision.

Open collaboration will be the key to its success.

We'll lead by example, doing business and science in a more local and open way.

Our company aims to be so open you'll always be next to the driver's seat, seeing and knowing 100% of our company's operations and its decisions.

Our style is barebones, our motto is 'screw ego', and our banking account is currently at $2,171.21.
